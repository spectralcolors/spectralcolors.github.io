Jack Lanchantin

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at Facebook AI Research (FAIR) in New York City, where I'm mentored by Arthur Szlam. I'm also fortunate to work closely with Sainbayar Sukhbaatar. I recently defended my PhD in computer science at the University of Virginia, where I was advised by Dr. Yanjun Qi (thesis slides available here). My research interests are in representation learning and interpretable models for sequence and graph structured data. In particular, I've focused on data from genomics, proteomics, images, and natural language. I previously spent time at Microsoft Research as a research intern in the Computer Vision Group.
